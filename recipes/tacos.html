Tacos

Picture a vibrant plate of freshly made tacos, each one a colorful and mouthwatering work of art. The soft, lightly charred corn tortillas are folded gently around a generous filling—juicy, spiced grilled meat or tender shredded chicken, still steaming.

Each taco is topped with a rainbow of fresh garnishes: diced red onions, chopped cilantro, slices of ripe avocado, and a sprinkle of crumbly white queso fresco. Bright red salsa drips slightly down the sides, hinting at bold, smoky flavors. A lime wedge rests nearby, ready to be squeezed, adding a final zesty note.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (or chicken, or tofu)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or mix of chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt)
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • ½ cup chopped onion (optional)
  • Salsa, sour cream, and avocado (optional toppings)

Steps

  1. Cook meat in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain fat.
  2. Add seasoning and ½ cup water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos: spoon meat into tortillas, add toppings as desired.
  5. Serve immediately with lime wedges and enjoy!
