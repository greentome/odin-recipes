Burger

Imagine a thick, juicy burger stacked high on a toasted sesame seed bun. The top bun is golden and slightly glossy, with a light sheen of melted butter. Below it, crisp green lettuce peeks out over a slice of ripe, red tomato. A perfectly melted slice of cheddar cheese oozes over the edges of a freshly grilled beef patty, still sizzling slightly with char marks.

Under the patty, there’s a layer of thinly sliced red onions and crunchy dill pickles, adding texture and tang. The bottom bun is sturdy but soft, just enough to soak up the juicy drippings without falling apart. A touch of ketchup and mayo can be seen peeking out from the sides, adding a creamy, tangy finish.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patties
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ketchup & mustard

Steps

  1. Cook on a grill or pan, 3–4 minutes per side (add cheese near the end).
  2. Toast burger buns lightly.
  3. Assemble: bun, lettuce, tomato, patty with cheese, onion, condiments, top bun.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
